What is NodeSchool?

NodeSchool is an open source project run by volunteers with two goals: to create high quality programming curriculum and to host community learning events.

Wellington Community

Wellington, the capital of New Zealand, is a vibrant city filled with lots of culture. People of all backgrounds come together to both learn from and mentor others.

All skill levels are encouraged to come participate in the weekly meetups. We are always happy to foster growth of those new to Node.js; at the same time, there is an open call for mentors to participate in sharing Node with others.

Get involved

Join us at one of the weekly meet-ups. Or, participate in NodeSchool Wellington'sonline discussions. Come learn, observe, mentor, and connect in a fun and friendly environment.
